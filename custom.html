I design and create every engagement ring and set of wedding bands for the specific couple.

I work in gold (14 or 18 karat), white-gold (14 or 18 karat) and 925 sterling silver. All the stones I use are either fairtrade diamonds or natural gemstones mined under sustainable conditions for both the miners, stone-cutters and the environment.

The price varies depending on design (width, thickness, size etc.) and material choice.

We will start by looking and trying on rings with different looks, surface structures and materials.
Together we will talk and sketch until we have designed a set of rings exactly as you wish. If you want a stone in one or both rings this is also decided on this meeting.

If you are unable to come by for a meeting in Copenhagen we can do it by email or phone.

If you have any questions or want to book a meeting, don't hesitate to send an email to hello@sarahwinther.com or use the contact formular below.
