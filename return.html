International Return
If a returned item is shipped international, please clearly mark the package 'RETURNED GOODS', to avoid any customs charges. Sarah Winther is not responsible for any return customs charges made. Any charges incurred are the responsibility of the customer.

Exchange

Want to switch to another product or another size of the same product?

Send an e-mail to return@sarahwinther.com named "Exchange" and provide your customer information, that you will send back the product and the product and size you wish to receive in exchange.

If the product is lost or you, upon receiving, notice that the item has been damaged during transport, you should immediately report this to Sarah Winther.

Unclaimed Packages

For packages that are not cashed at your post office within 14 days, Sarah Winther reserves the right to charge you a handling fee to cover return shipping and freight companies administrative costs.
